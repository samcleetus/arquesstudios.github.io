About Us

As a solo game developer, I founded Arques Studios — a passion project game development company — as a means through which to tell stories through gameplay. Every detail, from concept and code to design and marketing, is crafted with care to bring unique ideas to life.

I created Arques Studios as a space to explore creativity and deliver games that are immersive, stylish, and fun. Whether it's through imaginative worlds or charming characters, my goal is to create experiences that stay with players long after they've finished playing.

Ascata is just the beginning. I'm excited to keep building — one game at a time.

Ascata

Ascata is Arques Studios' debut mobile release -- an endless vertical platformer. With multiple immersive settings and a wide range of unlockable character skins, you’ll jump your way through a variety of visual worlds — from gear-filled factories to vibrant cityscapes and mysterious forests. Whether you're playing as an orange tabby or another stylish character, every run offers a fresh and exciting experience.

Download and play for free on the App Store!
