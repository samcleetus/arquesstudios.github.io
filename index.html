Ascata

Ascata is Arques Studios' debut mobile release! An endless vertical platformer with multiple immersive settings and a wide range of unlockable character skins, you'll jump your way through a variety of visual worlds, from gear-filled factories to vibrant cityscapes and mysterious forests. Whether you're playing as an orange tabby or another stylish character, every run offers a fresh and exciting experience.

Download and play for free on the App Store!

Coming Soon: King's Crest

King's Crest is Arques Studios' next major project; a story-driven first-person exploration game about solitude, perseverance, and truth. As a traveler climbing a mountain cloaked in secrecy, players uncover why the monarchs isolated themselves, and in the process, must confront the emotional and moral weight of their own journey.

Built with cutting-edge integration of large language models (LLMs), King's Crest encourages players to decipher clues and grant meanings by their own design. The choices you make and how you interpret what they find shape how the story ends. King's Crest represents our vision for the future of interactive storytelling, where human design and AI technology can be combined to create unforgettable experiences.

About

As a solo game developer, I founded Arques Studios as a means through which to build worlds and tell stories through gameplay. Every detail, from concept and code to design and marketing, is crafted with care to bring unique ideas to life.

Inspired by the Château d’Arques, a symbol of enduring craftsmanship, resilience, and artistry, I strive to create games built with the same precision and imagination. Whether through vibrant worlds or memorable characters, my goal is to craft stories that stay with players long after the game ends and experiences that stand the test of time

Ascata is just the beginning. I'm excited to keep building one game at a time.
