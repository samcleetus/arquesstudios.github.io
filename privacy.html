Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Arques Studios handles data within the Ascata mobile game.

Information We Collect

Ascata does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. The game is designed to run fully offline and does not require a user account or login.

Third-Party Services

At this time, Ascata does not use third-party services such as analytics, advertising, or cloud storage. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and list those services here.

Children’s Privacy

Ascata is suitable for players of all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If a parent or guardian believes that their child has provided personal information, they can contact us to request its removal.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the revised version on this page and update the “last updated” date below.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at arquesstudios@gmail.com.
